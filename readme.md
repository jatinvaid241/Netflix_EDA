## Overview
This project performs Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on the Netflix Movies and TV Shows dataset to uncover insights about content distribution, ratings, duration, release trends, and country-wise production.

## Dataset
- Dataset: Netflix Movies & TV Shows
- Source: Kaggle
- Format: CSV

## Tools & Technologies
- Python
- Pandas
- Matplotlib

## Key Analysis Performed
- Data cleaning and preprocessing
- Movies vs TV Shows distribution
- Content rating percentage analysis
- Movie duration distribution
- Release year vs number of shows
- Top 10 countries by content count
- Comparison of Movies and TV Shows over the years

## Visualizations
- Bar charts
- Pie charts
- Histogram
- Scatter plot
- Line charts

## Key Insights
- Netflix has more Movies than TV Shows, but TV Shows have grown rapidly in recent years
- Majority of content is targeted towards mature audiences
- Most movies have a duration between 80â€“120 minutes
- Content production increased significantly after 2015
- United States and India are among the top content-producing countries
